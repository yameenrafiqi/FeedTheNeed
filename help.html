How Can We Help You?

Welcome to our Help page! Below you’ll find answers to some of the most common questions. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out to us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is "Feed The Need"?

Feed The Need is a non-profit organization committed to reducing food waste by rescuing surplus food and redistributing it to those in need.

How can I donate food?

If you have surplus food to donate, simply get in touch with us via our Contact Us page, and we will arrange a pickup or drop-off location.

Can I volunteer with Feed The Need?

Yes! We are always looking for passionate volunteers. Visit our Volunteer page for more details on how to get involved.

How do I know if my donation was received?

Once your donation is received, we will send a confirmation email. You can also track your contribution on our website under your account.

Can I donate money instead of food?

Yes, we welcome monetary donations to help fund our operations. Please visit our Donate page for more details on how to contribute.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to Feed The Need are tax-deductible. We will provide you with a receipt for your records.
